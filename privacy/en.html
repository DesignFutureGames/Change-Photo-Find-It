1. Purposes & Data We Process

1) Purposes

  • Provide/operate the service: gameplay, saves, leaderboards/achievements, Google Play sign-in
  • Ads: interstitial ads via Google AdMob, frequency capping, basic measurement and fraud prevention
  • Purchases: verify and restore IAP (permanent ad removal)
  • Support: troubleshoot and respond to inquiries

2) Categories of Data

  • Identifiers/Device info: advertising ID (AAID), IP, OS/device model, app version, locale, display info
  • Usage/Events: stage, score, progress, taps, session timestamps, basic logs
  • Play Games (optional): nickname, profile image, player ID, achievements/scores
  • Purchase info (upon purchase): SKU, purchase time, receipt/token, status (refund/cancel)
  • Diagnostics: crash/performance data

3) How We Collect

Data is collected automatically through SDKs during app use, or provided by the user within the scope of consent where required.

2. Third Parties / Processors

We use the following providers. They may process/store data outside your country.

  • Google AdMob (Google LLC): interstitial ads and basic measurement (e.g., AAID, IP, device info, ad impression/click events)
  • Google Play Billing (Google LLC): in-app purchases (ad removal) (e.g., purchase token, SKU, transaction time/status)
  • Google Play Games Services (Google LLC): sign-in, leaderboards, achievements (e.g., player ID, nickname, profile image, scores/achievements)
  • Google UMP SDK: consent collection/management in EEA/UK and similar regions

See each provider’s privacy policy for details. We will notify changes to processors in this policy.

3. Legal Bases for Processing

  • Contract performance: core gameplay, leaderboards/achievements, purchase processing
  • Legitimate interests: service security, anti-fraud, basic quality improvement, non-personalized (contextual) ads
  • Consent: where required for personalized ads/measurement, collected via UMP; you can withdraw at any time

4. Retention & Deletion

  • Principle: delete without undue delay once the purpose is fulfilled
  • Legal retention (where applicable): certain e-commerce records (e.g., contract/purchase 5 years, disputes 3 years, advertising 6 months)
  • Destruction: secure deletion of electronic files; shredding/incineration for printouts

5. Your Choices & Controls

  • Consent management: in EEA/UK, manage consent via the UMP banner shown at first launch or in-app settings
  • Permanent Ad Removal (IAP): after purchase, the app is designed to stop requesting ads (a restart may be needed in some environments). Restore purchases on the same account
  • Google Play Games: sign-in is optional; you can play without it
